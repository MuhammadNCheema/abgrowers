Our Story

who are we ?

This is the introduction to M/s AB AgriGrow Enterprises as a fruit growing, handling, trading, and exporting firm in the heart of Pakistan’s best Kinnow growing region, Sargodha. The history of AB AgriGrow goes back to the early 1900’s where the founding family started commercial farming of fruits and vegetables. The introduction of Kinnow, a Mandarin variety in the middle of century presented an opportunity to produce world class quality fruit originating in the villages of Sargodha region. AB AgriGrow Enterprises was founded by Kinnow growers with farming experience that made the transition from farmers to producers, traders and exporters as natural outcome.

Our Standards

As pioneers in citrus farming in Sargodha region, the AB founding family has established quality Kinnow farms, and uses their own state of the art facility to clean, process, wax, grade and pack Kinnows for international and local markets. The process of growing Kinnow to reaching the consumer is strictly controlled to exceed international quality standards. The quality control mechanisms at AB are standardized by internationally trained directors and support staff. The AB family Kinnow growing fields extend over 500 Acres in the vicinity of the processing plant, Sargodha.
